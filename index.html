Welcome to the Fiesta

It's Happening.

The OG crew is back — older, arguably wiser, but definitely not more sensible.

What started on the shores of Tenerife all those years ago has led us here. The sunburns have faded. The hangovers are a distant (repressed) memory. But the vibes? The vibes are eternal.

Before our girl Fetty Trush trades her party hat for a wedding veil, we're doing this one more time — properly.

Three nights. One house. Zero excuses.

Pack your bags, charge your phones, and prepare for Tenerife 2.0.

This is a surprise for Trusha — she knows what to wear but not where we're going or what we're doing. Let's keep it that way. 🤫

Legends of Tenerife 1.0

The trip that started it all. The stories we'll never live down.

🕵️

The Case of the Missing Diren

One minute he was there. The next — gone. Vanished. Search party assembled. Panic levels rising. Where was he? On the beach. Just... on the beach. Vibing. Alone. Living his best life while the rest of us lost ours looking for him.

💍

Kishen's Ring Saga

Kishen and the beach have unfinished business. Somewhere in the sands of Tenerife, his ring remains — buried treasure for a future generation to discover. A moment of silence for the fallen jewellery. 🥺

😭

The One Who Almost Didn't Make It

Priya wasn't allowed to come on the Tenerife trip. So naturally, Trusha and Anika turned up at her house to convince her dad in person. A valiant effort. An impassioned pitch. He said no. The negotiations were unsuccessful. But the spirit of trying? Legendary. Priya — you made it to 2.0. That's what matters.

Fiesta Rules

Non-negotiable. No exceptions. (Okay, maybe some exceptions.)

01 What happens at the Fiesta stays at the Fiesta. Unless it's funny enough to make the group chat.
02 Trusha's glass must never be empty. If you see it below half, you know what to do.
03 When "We No Speak Americano" plays, you dance. No exceptions. No sitting down. No pretending you don't know the moves.
04 Diren must remain visible at all times. We are NOT doing another beach search party. Buddy system if necessary.
05 Kishen — keep your jewellery inside. The house has no beach. You're welcome.
